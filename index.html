Buch Oil

Buch Oil is a section of Buch Mineral Exploration Company and it is focused on the exploration of shale oil.
Buch Oil carries out every oil process which includes the global processes of exploration, extraction, refining, transportation, and marketing of petroleum products. The largest volume products of the industry are fuel oil and gasoline.

Services

These are the services Buch Oil renders under the house name of Buch Mineral Exploration Company as it is a sub company of Buch Mineral Exploration Company

Buch Oil renders two main service

ONE

  • Geophysical survey
  • Geological mapping

TWO

  1. Extraction of mineral(oil)
  2. Purification of mineral(oil)
